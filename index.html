PDF Format Checker

Checks the formatting rules in the Call for Papers. The exact checker the chairs run: thresholds, command line, and sources in the repository.

Pre-release prototype. This checker is meant to help authors and chairs, not to gatekeep: it measures the rendered PDF and can have false positives, and the submission form will include a text field to briefly explain any finding that does not apply to your paper. Comments and feedback are very welcome: jo.vanbulck@kuleuven.be.
